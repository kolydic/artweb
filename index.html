Section 1 Assignment 1


the system:
1. freewrite to the prompt "what does it feel like to be a human in 2026?"
2. displace your text with an ASCII image that reflects the writing.

the process
I chose an image of a mushroom and used one of the provided image-to-ASCII conversion tools. I then placed this in the file and arranged the content. Finally, I used an online text-to-binary conversion tool on a stripped version of the freewriting text and styled this as a "background."

Version 2

I manually drew a 'binary tree,' that is, the text as branching where binary codes of letters diverge.
